Candidate Safety Advisory

Read our essential guidelines to prevent fraud, fake job offers, and ensure a secure international career transition.

Our Commitment to Transparency

Trendy Fortune Global Pvt. Ltd. operates strictly under government guidelines. We do not engage with third-party sub-agents, and all communications are routed through authorized office coordinators. Below are critical rules you must follow:

01

No Upfront Fees

We do not request cash or advance payments for visa processing, interviews, or registrations. Report any agent asking for cash immediately.

02

Direct Employer Validation

Before committing to work contracts or travel, ensure you verify the salary scale, role description, and employer details through direct official contacts.

03

Official Channels Only

Only correspond with email addresses ending with @trendyfortune.com. Do not send credentials or money to personal gmail accounts.

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Agencies guaranteeing a visa 100% without verification.
  • Requesting money transfers to personal bank accounts.
  • Refusal to provide license numbers or corporate registration codes.

Safe Steps to Take

  • Ask to see the official Ministry License number.
  • Always visit the registered branch offices in person.
  • Check feedback and verify employers directly on embassy portals.
